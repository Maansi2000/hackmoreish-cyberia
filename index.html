  • Golden Rule: Practice Pause & Stay Suspicious

    • 1

      You got a mail by the name of a friend or colleague asking for urgent money transfer.

      What would you do?

    • 2

      And what if there's also an active link attached in such a mail.

      Then what?

    • 3

      If you didn't act on it without pausing to think, you might start to feel something is off.

      Process begins...

    • 4

      A person who lives nearby or with whom you can have phone conversations chose this method of communication.

      Strange.

    • 5

      If money was asked in the form of gift cards. Gift cards are meant for gifts only.

      Stranger.

    • 6

      So finally, you can check with the person directly with phone and other contact information you have. Don't interact with the mail before that.

      Especially don't click any links.
